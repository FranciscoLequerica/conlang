# Artistic Philosophy

## Vision
The artistic philosophy for our conlang project is an exploration of language as an art form. We aim to create a conlang that not only serves as a means of communication but also embodies a deep aesthetic and cultural resonance. Inspired by Francisco Lequerica's artistic practice, we recognize language as a dynamic entity that evolves, reflects human emotions, and interacts with sociocultural contexts.

## Constraints
1. **Cultural Context**: The conlang must respect and reflect the cultural backgrounds of its speakers, incorporating idioms, folklore, and social norms.
2. **Phonetic Authenticity**: Following the principles derived from the Saulo y la entropía column, the phonetic structure should prioritize naturalistic sounds that align with global linguistic diversity and intuitiveness.
3. **Artistic Integrity**: Every element of the conlang must approach its creation with artistic intentions, valuing beauty and expression alongside functionality.
4. **Iterative Process**: Embrace the inherent entropy of language development. Revision and evolution should be seen as part of the creative process rather than constraints.

## Approach
1. **Inspiration from Existing Languages**: Study various languages to draw upon phonetic patterns, morphological structures, and syntactic uniqueness. This will create a rich tapestry of influences that honor linguistic history.
2. **Collaborative Development**: Engage with a community of artists and linguists to foster an environment of shared creativity, thereby broadening the scope of input and expression.
3. **Phonetic Experimentation**: Explore sound combinations and constructs that challenge conventional norms while remaining accessible and appealing to speakers.
4. **Cultural Narratives**: Infuse the language with stories and myths that resonate with potential users, ensuring the conlang feels like a living, breathing part of a culture.
5. **Reflection and Feedback**: Regularly seek feedback from users to refine the language, keeping pace with its evolving nature and ensuring it remains artistically vibrant.

The development of this conlang is an ongoing journey, one that ultimately seeks to deepen the connection between language, culture, and art, reflecting the very essence of human expression.
